Our Story

Vagabond Studios was originally born as a part time venture and passion project while Ryan was living and working on yachts overseas. Based on recent demand, the studio has evolved to develop a specialization in website design, and teaming up with Catherine brought together two designers with a shared experience of over 40 years within the field of visual communications. We are passionate about the importance of great design, and we love helping organizations feel good about their brand. Although Catherine and Ryan have spent many stints abroad and on the move 'vagabonding' in years past, today we are proudly based in the city of Chicago. Because we operate as a small, independent studio, we can offer quality deliverables at a reasonable cost.
