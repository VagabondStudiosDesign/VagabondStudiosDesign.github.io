Chicago Metro Map

TRANSIT MAP / DIAGRAM OF CTA & COMMUTER TRAINS
This (Unofficial) Map Design was created to combine and simplify all of the various train routes in the greater Chicago area including The EL, Metra, & South Shore Line. The diagram is streamlined to focus on urban rail exploration in the city and beyond, and how to navigate connections between the various hubs, routes, and operators.

SEE THE FULL MAP →

New York Metro Map

TRANSIT MAP / DIAGRAM OF GREATER NEW YORK
The complexity involved in the Greater New York Transit systems is immeasurable. While designers tend to love the Vingelli map more than the average New York commuter, this map was conceived with a middle ground in mind. There is more geographic accuracy integrated while still simplifying angles to go easy on the brain, with a meticulous attention in the details of transfer stations and connections. 

SEE THE FULL MAP →

About:

Vagabond Studios was originally born as a part time venture and passion project while Ryan was living and working on yachts overseas. Inspired by the art and history of his experience abroad, many project ideas began to take shape in the world of cartography and design, and in recent years these projects were brought to life in the ever-evolving digital sphere. After 15 years of vagabonding with a laptop in a variety of cafes, airbnbs, and tiny cabins below deck, today the studio is proudly based in the city of Chicago.
